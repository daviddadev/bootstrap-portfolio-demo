Hi, I'm David Soto

Hi, My name is David - I'm a San Diego based military veteran who found his bona fide devotion as a Front-end Web Developer. Not too long ago I began teaching myself how to program. The moment I wrote my first JavaScript "Hello World" app, I became hooked!

I love the fact that I can be creative, build awesome websites, and solve problems simultaneously. The more I would explore the world of coding the more enthusiastic I would become. I love to learn new things, and technology has plenty to feed my aptness to keep on learning.

When I'm not writing code or learning some new tech, I'm usually outdoors exploring the city with my family, taking photos, surfing at the beach, or eating tasty food in Mexico.
